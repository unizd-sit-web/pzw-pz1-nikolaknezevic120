About us

Welcome to the pages of Memories apartments located in St. Peter on the Sea near Zadar. On our website you can find out all the important information related for renting one or more apartments. Apartments are at your disposal sizes from 40m2 to 80m2.
Memories apartments were built in 2018 and are completely new equipped with a beautiful garden and peaceful surroundings. They are located near Zadar and Biograd na Moru, and are perfect for quiet summer vacation with your family.

Sveti Petar na moru -
St. Peter on the Sea

St. Peter on the Sea was named after the church of St. Peter the Apostle from the 18th century. In the center of the town you can see the remains of medieval outbuildings which belonged to the abbey on Rogovo.
Once an exclusively peasant place, Saint Today, Petar na Moru is increasingly turning to tourism. After the Homeland War and the collapse of the processing industry, but also individual agricultural production, the former farmers of St. Peter on the Sea turn to tourism as the only one profitable industry.
